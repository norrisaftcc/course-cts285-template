## PART 1: CREATIVE BRIEF OVERVIEW

### Project Overview

Design a complete brand identity and marketing system for **Ride**, a vehicle comparison platform that helps car shoppers track features across test drives and make confident decisions without dealer pressure. Unlike Consumer Reports data overload or dealer recommendations serving their commission, Ride gives buyers a systematic comparison tool that cuts through the noise and keeps them in control during the high-pressure buying process.

Students will create white-label branding for a recommendation engine platform that combines a **preference-learning app** with a **physical comparison worksheet pad**. The challenge is designing for buyers (first-time, upgrade, family car) who feel overwhelmed by choices, anxious about dealer manipulation, and can‚Äôt remember which features matter after test-driving multiple vehicles.

## PART 2: PRODUCT DEEP DIVE

### What Ride Is

A vehicle comparison platform that helps you define what matters (safety, space, tech, budget), track those priorities across multiple test drives, and compare vehicles systematically so you can make confident decisions without dealer pressure. Unlike review sites giving opinions or data sites overwhelming with specs, Ride focuses on YOUR priorities and captures details in the moment‚Äîduring test drives when information is fresh and dealers are watching.
